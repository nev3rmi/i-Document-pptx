Overview: Solar, Wind, and Hydro Power

Solar, wind, and hydro are the leading renewable energy sources, providing about 29% of the world's electricity. Each technology harnesses natural forces to generate clean power, supporting sustainable energy goals globally.

Solar Power

Uses photovoltaic cells to turn sunlight into electricity.

Wind Power

Transforms the kinetic energy of wind into electrical energy.

Hydro Power

Generates power by directing flowing water through turbines.
